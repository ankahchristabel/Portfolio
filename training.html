Training

STL/EC GHANA

The IT Technician Training program offered by STL/EC Ghana is a comprehensive initiative aimed at equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge required to excel in the field of information technology (IT) support and maintenance. The program covers a wide range of topics, including hardware and software troubleshooting, network configuration and administration, system maintenance, and security protocols.

Participants in the training program undergo rigorous hands-on training sessions, practical exercises, and real-world simulations to develop proficiency in IT technical skills. The curriculum is designed by industry experts and is regularly updated to align with the latest technological advancements and industry best practices.
